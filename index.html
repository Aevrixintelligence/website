How it works

Simple by design,
powerful by default

Four steps from raw data to actionable intelligence.

01
Connect your data

Any source — live feeds, video files, image datasets, or APIs. No preprocessing or formatting required.

02
AI processes everything

Every frame, image, and second of footage is analysed by our on-device vision model and indexed for instant retrieval.

03
Ask in plain language

Type any question. Get precise answers with timestamps, clips, and evidence — in seconds, not hours.

04
Automate & alert

Set conditions in plain language. The system monitors 24/7 and fires the moment anything matches — with evidence attached.
